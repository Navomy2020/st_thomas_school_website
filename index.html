ABOUT US

Welcome to
St. Thomas LP School Kangazha

Established in 1919, St. Thomas LP School Kangazha is a Private Aided primary school located in the rural area of Kangazha, Kottayam, Kerala. We provide quality education from Grade 1 to Grade 4 in Malayalam medium while nurturing young minds with strong values and bright futures.

Our Vision

To create a nurturing environment that empowers every child to reach their full potential.

Our Mission

To provide quality education, instill values and promote holistic development.
